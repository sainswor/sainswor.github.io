Exit Strategy

by Rades on September 9, 2015 at 9:37 am
Chapter: Chapter 6: Anger

A few people were asking what Rades was pouring out of that bag back on Page #80 — I told you it’d be explained! Granted, maybe he originally intended to use it just as a distraction, but I think it still served its purpose. (We also love seeing your comments trying to predict things that haven’t been revealed or explained yet in the story — we always get a kick when you guess right!)

As for this page…sometimes when I send Vid the comic storyboard I get some good-natured groans if it’s a particularly complex layout. I really don’t know what came over me with this one. But Vid’s a champ and nailed it, like always. 😀
